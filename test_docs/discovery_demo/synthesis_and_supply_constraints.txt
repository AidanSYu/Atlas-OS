Synthesis and supply constraints (mock)

Ordering preferences:
- Prefer make-on-demand catalog compounds with <= 4 week lead time.
- Favor compounds with listed catalog IDs for rapid procurement.

Synthesis complexity limits:
- Target route length <= 4 steps for primary queue.
- Avoid chemotypes requiring unstable intermediates or cryogenic-only handling.
- Penalize compounds with no plausible retrosynthetic route.

Scale assumptions:
- Initial order: 5 mg per hit for biochemical confirmation.
- Follow-up scale: 25-50 mg for in vitro and ADME panel.

Budget constraints:
- Per-compound soft cap: USD 300 for initial procurement.
- High-priority exceptions allowed if potency and safety profile are strong.

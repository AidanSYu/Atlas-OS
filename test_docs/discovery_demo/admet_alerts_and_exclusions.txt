ADMET Triage Guide for Demo Runs

Hard exclusions:
- Predicted hERG high risk
- Flagged reactive nitroso motifs
- Strong mutagenicity alert in aromatic nitro clusters

Soft penalties:
- CYP3A4 inhibition: medium or high
- Caco2 permeability: low
- Predicted aqueous solubility < 0.05 mg/mL

Known poor motifs:
- Naphthalene-heavy cores (high hERG signal frequency)
- Unsubstituted aniline nitro motifs
- Highly basic dialkyl amines with cationic persistence

Preferred ADMET window:
- hERG risk: low
- DILI risk: low or medium
- Caco2 permeability: medium or high
- Half-life target: 2-8 hours (avoid very long accumulation)

Campaign note: KRAS-Alpha-02

Summary of prior round:
- 126 compounds synthesized from a benzothiophene-acrylamide starter series.
- 14 compounds reached biochemical IC50 < 500 nM.
- 4 compounds reached cellular EC50 < 1500 nM.

Observed liabilities:
- Naphthalene-rich variants increased hERG liabilities.
- Highly lipophilic analogs (LogP > 5.2) showed low solubility and erratic PK.
- Nitro-aniline substitutions correlated with Ames-positive signals in external panel.

Scaffold guidance:
- Keep covalent warhead placement at para orientation where possible.
- Bias toward heteroaromatic replacements to reduce cLogP while preserving potency.
- Retain one hydrogen bond acceptor near the hinge-facing region.

Do not repeat:
- Fused bicyclic hydrophobes with no polar exit vectors.
- Poly-halogenated tails with poor clearance.

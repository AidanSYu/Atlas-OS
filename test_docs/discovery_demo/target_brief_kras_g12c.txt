Project: KRAS G12C Covalent Inhibitor Sprint
Owner: Discovery Demo Team
Date: 2026-03-10

Primary objective:
- Identify small molecules that bind KRAS G12C at the switch-II pocket.
- Prioritize reversible or targeted covalent chemotypes with oral exposure potential.

Target profile:
- Target: KRAS G12C GDP-preferred state
- Mechanistic intent: suppress MAPK pathway signaling in KRAS G12C cell models

Initial property constraints:
- Molecular weight <= 500
- LogP between 1.5 and 4.5
- TPSA <= 120
- Hydrogen bond donors <= 3
- Rotatable bonds <= 9

Safety constraints:
- Avoid high hERG risk predictions
- Prefer low DILI risk
- Penalize strong CYP3A4 inhibition alerts

Success criteria (screening phase):
- Biochemical IC50 < 1000 nM
- Cellular EC50 < 3000 nM
- No high-risk structural alerts (Brenk/toxicophore)
